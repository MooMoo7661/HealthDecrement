This mod will decrease your max hp every time you die. It has configurable amounts, and also includes commands.


HOW TO USE THIS MOD:
Go into the config and change the amount of max hp you want to lose per death, or leave it the same.
Type /help into the in-game chat to see the commands available

Commands:

"resethp"
Resets the counter for how much of your max HP is currently being taken

"lockhp"
Locks your max HP from being affected per death

"unlockhp"
Unlocks your max HP from being affected per death

"settakenhp"
Sets the counter for how much of your max HP is currently being taken
